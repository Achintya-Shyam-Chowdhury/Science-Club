About Us

Welcome to the Research Club at Rajnagar Technical School & College! We are a school-based AI research club dedicated to fostering curiosity, creativity, and collaboration among students interested in artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to explore and expand their knowledge of AI through hands-on projects, workshops, and discussions.
