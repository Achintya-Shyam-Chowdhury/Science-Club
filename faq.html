Why was this website created?

Ans: This website was created to help our fellow students learn various domains and many other things that will help them in the future. It aims to make their lives better compared to the past.

How can I join the club?

Ans: We recognize every student of the school as a member of our club. However, if you are interested in collaborating with us and working on amazing projects, please inform one of the admins or any teacher. It would be best if you also inform our Vice President or Secretary.

What is the goal of this club?

Ans: The initial goal of this club is to make students excel in science, math, and technology, and change their perspective on the world. It also aims to help students create more appropriate science fair projects and complete them before the event.
